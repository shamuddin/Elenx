## 3. The "Investor-Grade" MVP Features

### **3.1 The Self-Healing Kernel**
*   **Problem:** Websites change (A/B tests, layout shifts), causing 40% of agents to fail.
*   **Solution:** When the "Executor Agent" hits a 404 or missing element, the **Self-Healing Kernel** pulls a structured "Golden Schema" from the **Bright Data Web Scraper API** to re-orient the agent.

### **3.2 The Behavioral Mimicry Engine**
*   **Problem:** Anti-bots (DataDome, Cloudflare) flag "too efficient" agents.
*   **Solution:** Injects **"Human Jitter"**—micro-hesitations, non-linear scrolling, and erratic mouse paths—into the **Scraping Browser** session.

### **3.3 The Zero-Trust Intent Sandbox**
*   **Problem:** Semantic Compliance Hijacking (SCH) via hidden web instructions.
*   **Solution:** An MCP-based "Check-and-Balance" system. No action is executed until the **Verifier Agent** confirms it aligns with the **User's Root Intent.**
