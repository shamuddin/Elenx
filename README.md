## 🛠 Three Pillars of Sovereignty

### 1. B2C: Personal Sovereignty (Consumer)
ELENX protects personal wallets and identities from **Goal Hijacking**. Using **zkSIP (Zero-Knowledge Proofs)** and **Bright Data ISP Proxies**, we allow personal assistants to buy groceries or book travel without ever exposing private credentials or being redirected to malicious "Buy" traps.

### 2. B2B: The Agentic Enterprise (Enterprise)
ELENX solves the **Supply Chain Poisoning** problem. With our **Lineage DAG**, we trace the origin of every piece of data. If an agent reads a poisoned blog post via **Exa Search** that tries to trigger a malicious **Shell Command**, our **Lineage Guard** instantly freezes the action and requests human approval.

### 3. B2G: Sovereign Compliance (Government)
ELENX is the mandatory **System of Record** for the **EU AI Act** and **FedRAMP 2026**. We maintain a **Hash-Chained Transactional Ledger** (Flight Recorder 2.0) that dual-signs every agent action, providing immutable, legally admissible proof of autonomous intent.
